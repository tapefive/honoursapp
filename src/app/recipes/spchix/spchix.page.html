Salt & Pepper Chicken
Ingredients 8 diced chicken breasts

6 spring onions, thinly sliced

2 red finger chilli, thinly sliced

2 garlic cloves, crushed

2 tbsp Chinese five-spice powder

2 tbsp fine salt

120g cornflour

2 tbsp ground black pepper

400ml vegetable oil Method 1. In a large bowl, mix the cornflour, salt, five-spice and tablespoon of pepper together and set aside

2. Heat the oil in a wok or large pan to 180C on a cooking thermometer, or until a teaspoon of cornflour sizzles when dropped in

(CAUTION: Hot oil can be dangerous. Do not leave unattended)

3. Toss the chicken in the flour mixture, working in batches

4. Carefully add it to the oil and cook for 2–3 minutes on one side, until golden brown and cooked though

5. Turn and cook for a further 2–3 minutes. Drain on kitchen paper

6. Heat the remaining 1 tablespoon of oil in a clean pan over a medium heat

7. Add the spring onions, chilli, garlic and remaining teaspoon of pepper with a pinch of salt

8. Fry for 1–2 minutes, then add the cooked chicken

9. Toss and cook for a further 2–3 minutes, until the chicken is coated in the onions, garlic and chilli

10. Serve immediately with chips or fried rice

Serves 4
