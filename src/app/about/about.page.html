About Us Who We Are The Dumfries & Galloway Asian Food Company was established in September 2019 with the aim of providing Asian food products and ingredients to local residents and International students studying in the area via an easy to use mobile application.

As well as products and ingredients, the application also provides users with a variety of simple recipes to inspire and help people broaden their culinary horizons with the products and ingredients we sell. What We Sell A survey was conducted before the application was developed to establish what kind of products potential users would like to see sold within the application.

As you can see from the chart above, the survey results show that Sauces was the most popular choice. The types of sauces we sell include a variety of Chilli, Hoisin, Soy, Plum, Teriyake and Satay sauces.

The second most popular choice was Won Ton & Dumplings. We sell a variety of flavours including Pork & Chive, Prawn, Chicken and Vegetable frozen dumplings. We also sell dumpling wrappers for people who want to make their own at home.
