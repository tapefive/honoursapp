Pork & Chive Dumplings
Ingredients Filling

500g pork mince

½ cup finely chopped chives

¼ tsp sesame oil

2 tsp Haitian Premium Wei Ji Xian Soy Sauce

1 tsp salt

1 tsp ground black pepper

1 packet of Imperial Dragon Gyoza Wrappers (Dumpling Skin) 284g

1 tbsp vegetable oil, to cook dumplings

2 tbsp cornflour

Dipping Sauce

2 tsp rice wine

1 tsp sesame oil

1 tsp Haitian Premium Wei Ji Xian Soy Sauce

1 tsp toasted sesame seeds

1 tsp dried chilli flakes

Method 1. In a large bowl, combine the dumpling filling ingredients and mix well

2. To wrap dumplings; place half a tablespoon of filling in the centre of each dumpling wrapper

3. Fold the circle wrapper in half and secure the sides together with a dab of water

4. Form a base on the dumpling as you stand each dumpling with the seam facing up

5. Continue filling the wrappers until the pork mince is finished

6. Lightly dust the dumplings with a little cornflour to ensure the dumplings don’t stick to each other

7. Heat vegetable oil in a frying pan over medium-high heat

8. Place dumplings in the pan and cook for 3-4 mins to allow the bottoms to colour

9. Bring a kettle of water to the boil and reduce heat of the pan with dumplings

10. CAUTION: Be very careful as you slowly pour the boiling water over the dumplings until the water is ⅓ of the way up the dumplings

11. Cover the pan with a lid and allow the water to completely evaporate. This will take approximately 7 minutes

12. Meanwhile, prepare the dipping sauce and set aside

13. When dumplings are cooked, remove from pan and arrange on a serving plate.

14. Serve immediately with the dipping sauce

Serves 4
