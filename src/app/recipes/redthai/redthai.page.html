Red Thai Chicken Curry
Ingredients 2 tablespoons of Mae Ploy Red Curry Paste

350g sliced chicken

200ml coconut milk

2 teaspoons of garlic puree

4 slices of fresh ginger

1 tablespoon of Thai fish sauce

2 tablespoons of sesame oil

100ml of chicken stock

2 lime leaves (shredded)

1/2 red pepper & 1/2 green pepper

2 tablespoons sugar

1 finely chopped red chilli to garnish Method 1. In a small saucepan warm up the coconut milk over a medium heat

2. Meanwhile in a wok add the sesame oil and heat until very hot

3. Swirl the hot oil around the wok then add the garlic. Stir fry for about 10-20 seconds until it begins to brown

4. Add the curry paste, stir fry for 10 seconds then add the warm coconut milk

5. Cook the coconut milk mixture for abut 5 minutes until it begins to thicken

6. Stir in the fish sauce, fresh ginger and sugar

7. Add the sliced chicken and cook for another 5 minutes or until all the chicken has turned white

8. Stir in the red and green peppers

9. Pour in the chicken stock, bring to the boil and simmer gently for 2 minutes

10. Stir in the shredded lime leaves

11. Turn into a warm serving dish, garnish with chopped red chilli

12. Serve immediately with steamed or boiled rice

Serves 4
